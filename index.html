01 — Who

SIDDHARTH
PANDEY

Siddharth Pandey is a Grade XII student from Southwest Delhi, currently at Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 2. He is not particularly invested in what the school means. He is very invested in what comes after it.

He watches a Formula One race the way an engineer watches a race — not for the drama, but for the strategy. The undercut, the tyre delta, the moment the race engineer says "box" and the whole thing pivots. That's the job he wants.

The path is mapped out. VIT Vellore for engineering. Pravega Racing for the first taste of competition. MRF Motorsport or TVS for real trackside experience. A master's at Cranfield or Oxford Brookes. And then the paddock — Milton Keynes or Maranello, wherever the work takes him.

He has not made it yet. He's going to.

Writes code the way a race engineer thinks — systematically, with a clear objective. Software is another kind of machine. You build it, you test it, you find out what breaks under pressure.

Puts words together with the same care he puts into everything else. Writing is thinking made visible — and he thinks carefully. About racing, about engineering, about the distance between Delhi and Maranello.
